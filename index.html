Madmaan is a dynamically and strong typed language that simplifies JavaScript's syntax and looks mad, MAN. Hate semi-colons? Well, try out exclamation marks! Does using "!" for "not" not make sense? Okay, use "NOT" instead, MAN. Plus, we know how ugly { } can be. SO. NO. CURLY. BRACKETS, MAN. This language explodes(compiles) into JavaScript, now go get MAD, MAAN.

madmaan is a programming language born from the minds of 7 students who are in a constant search to acquire more street cred (and also perhaps a decent GPA). Mitchell, Alex, Daniel Mondo, Arash, Anthony and Nick based madmaan off of Javascript, striving to make it easier to read. Also, we understand that writing code may not be the most exciting or pleasing thing you could do with a computer therefore we also decided to make the language more "fun" to write.

The idea of making madmaan sound like a disgrunt- I mean "mad" per- I mean "man" came after we thought of the name, which if you look closely was based off of the first letters of each of our names combined (those absolute madmen!).
